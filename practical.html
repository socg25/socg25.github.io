Useful Information
  • Visit Japan Web (VJW)
    • All individuals entering Japan are encouraged to register for the "Visit Japan Web (VJW)" online pre-registration service for immigration clearance and customs declaration.
    • Please register after purchasing your flight ticket and before your scheduled arrival time in Japan; otherwise, your QR code will not be accepted at Japanese airports.
    • Although pre-registration is not mandatory, it will save you a considerable amount of time upon arrival in Japan. If your travel schedule changes, you must update the information you registered.
  • Getting to Kanazawa: The following web pages provide a nice overview and details on how to get to Kanazawa.
    • ishikawatravel.jp
    • japan-guide.com
    • visitkanazawa.jp
